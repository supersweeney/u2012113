In a world where the planet is dying people are always looking for new days to help the planet and the environment. Many people do not realise that one of the key ways of doing this is preventing the sheer amount of energy they use in their day to day lives. It is common for people to waste electricty all the time. This can be from simple things like leaving devices on in stanby mode, not turning electronics off at the plug or perhaps even leaving lights on. This website's goal is to make people more aware of the implications of leaving their lights turned on when not needed, but also to inform the public of what energy saving bulbs are and how they can help both the consumer and the environment.

Please use the headings that are available at the top of the screen to navigate this website. You can find all the information on the different types of energy saving bulbs that are avaialable with some helpful links to purchase them. You will find the benefits to both yourself and the environment if you maintain good practice and turn your lights out when they are not required and if you use energy saving bulbs. Finally there will be a page where you can contact the makers of this website if you have any further questions or comments on the website.
